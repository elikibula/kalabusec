About Kalabu Secondary School

Established in 2004, Kalabu Secondary School is a proud Fijian institution where strength is built through resilience and dreams are shaped by determination. Our students come from diverse and humble beginnings, yet they rise above challenges with courage, unity, and an unwavering commitment to education. At Kalabu, we believe that greatness is not defined by where you start, but by the passion, discipline, and purpose you carry forward. Every student is empowered to grow, to lead, and to strive for excellence—turning ambition into achievement and potential into success.
